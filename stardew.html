Stardew

Valley

Stardew is a farming simulator made by Eric Barone which features you, the player, as a farmer who inherited their grandfather's farming land. The game is a wonderfully created passion project with plenty of personality in each aspect of the game.

Cultivate a variety of crops

Attend giant festivals

Explore the abandoned mines

My Rating

8.5/10

Stardew Valley is a very relaxing game and it is perfect for when the day is boring and you need to relax. The farming feels rewarding and there are so many other aspects to the game that give it so much life.
